Is someone there?

Ready for a Rock, paper, scissor, lizard, Spock match?

... time to go to the console

This game is played in the console. On Windows/ Linux press CTRL shift J to open.

RULES

  • It's very simple.
  • Scissors cuts paper,
  • paper covers rock,
  • crushes lizard,
  • lizard poisons Spock,
  • Spock smashes scissors,
  • scissors decapitates lizard,
  • lizard eats paper,
  • paper disproves Spock,
  • Spock vaporizes rock,
  • and as it always has,
  • rock crushes scissors.
